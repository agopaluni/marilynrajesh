## üñºÔ∏è Updating the Hero Section (Homepage)

**To edit your intro text:**
1. Click "Hero Section" in the sidebar
2. Edit "Main Headline" - This is the big, bold first line
3. Edit "Sub-headline" - This is the smaller text below it
4. Click "Publish" (green button at the bottom)

**To change your main photo:**
1. Click on the hero image box
2. Click "Upload" and select your photo
3. Adjust the crop/focus point by dragging the focal point
4. Click "Publish"

**To add floating images:**
1. Scroll to "Floating Images"
2. Click "+ Add item"
3. Upload each image (maximum 3)
4. Click "Publish"

**Changes appear instantly** on your website - no code needed!

## ‚úçÔ∏è Managing Your Writing Portfolio

**To add a new article:**
1. Click "Writing Section" in the sidebar
2. Scroll to "Writing Pieces"
3. Click "+ Add item"
4. Fill in:
   - **Article Title** - Name of your piece
   - **Short Description** - 1-2 sentences about it
   - **Tags** - Topics like "Journalism", "Fiction" (press Enter after each)
   - **Article Link** - Full URL where people can read it
   - **Cover Image (Optional)** - A photo for the article
5. Click "Publish"

**To reorder articles:**
- Drag and drop items using the ‚ãÆ‚ãÆ handle on the left
- Your preferred order appears on the website

**To edit an article:**
- Click on it in the list
- Make your changes
- Click "Publish"

**To delete an article:**
- Click on it
- Click the trash icon
- Confirm deletion

## üì∏ Managing Your Photography Gallery

**To add photos:**
1. Click "Photography Section"
2. Scroll to "Photo Gallery"
3. Click "+ Add item"
4. Upload your photo
5. Add a caption (optional)

7. Click "Publish"

**To reorder photos:**

- The gallery updates automatically

**To remove a photo:**
- Click the X button on the photo
- Click "Publish"

**Pro tip:** Add 6-15 photos for the best visual effect.

## üì∑ Image Guidelines

**For best results:**
- **Hero photo:** Portrait orientation (taller than wide)
- **Gallery photos:** Landscape orientation works best
- **File size:** Keep under 2MB each
- **Format:** JPG or PNG works fine

**Before uploading:**
- Use your phone or computer to crop/edit photos
- Make sure they're bright and clear
- Check that they're oriented correctly

## üÜò Getting Help

**If something doesn't work:**
1. Refresh your browser
2. Make sure you clicked "Publish"
3. Wait 2-3 minutes for changes to appear
4. Check that you're editing the right section

**If images don't upload:**
1. Make sure the file is under 10MB
2. Try a JPG or PNG format
3. Check your internet connection

**If you're stuck:**
- Take a screenshot of what you see
- Note which section you're editing
- Ask your developer or technical contact
